William Trouleau

Ph.D. student in Machine Learning

EPFL

Hi! I’m William. I’m a Ph.D. student at EPFL in the INDY Lab, under the supervision of Matthias Grossglauser and Patrick Thiran.

My research ranges from machine learning algorithms to probabilistic modeling. In particular, I am interested in the statistical and algorithmic aspects of modeling, control and inference of epidemic processes diffusing over networks ; with applications on disease modeling, information diffusion, and recommendation systems.
